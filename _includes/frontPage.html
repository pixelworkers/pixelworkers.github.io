Pixel Workers Meetup Event 0-4 is coming up next Tuesday, February 28. Come on out to the Toad ‘n’ Turtle for a beer and good pixel worker conversation. As always, first pint is on us!

Award-winning agricultural reporter and board chair of Volunteer Central Jennifer Blair, will give a 10-minute Lightening Talk on how volunteering benefits creative professionals and the community, how to find the right volunteer opportunity, and why volunteering is essential for personal and professional development.


Jennifer Blair is an award-winning agricultural reporter, communicator, and writer who has spent more than half her career telling stories. Jennifer has worked in a variety of communications and policy development roles, including at The City of Red Deer, Servus Credit Union, and Alberta Pulse Growers, before joining Alberta Farmer Express as a reporter in 2013. An avid volunteer, Jennifer has volunteered with 4-H, the Red Deer Festival of Trees, and Volunteer Central, where she currently acts as Board Chair.

ABOUT

The Pixel Workers meet-up is a once a month event where the Central Alberta creative industry can get together, share ideas, have a beer, and unite ourselves so we can all become better, together.

This is a group for designers, developers, writers, photographers, marketers, and anyone who works with pixels for a living, to find solace in a community that’s virtually non-existent in Red Deer today.

The format is simple. We get together one evening a month, hear a 10-15 minute lightening talk from a member of our community, and we spend the rest of the evening networking, hanging out, and using the presentation’s topic as an icebreaker for people to meet each other.

We really hope you’ll join us.

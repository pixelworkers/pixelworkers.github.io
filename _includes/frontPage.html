ABOUT

The Pixel Workers meet-up is a place where creative professionals in Central Alberta can get together to share ideas, have a beer or two, and rejoice in the fact that there are indeed more of us out there! The format is pretty simple: once a month we get together for an evening of networking. Each event will have a topic, kicking the night off with a quick 10-15 minute chat about said topic. From there, it’s time to drink some beer and share some thoughts!

Be it designers, developers, writers, photographers, marketers, and anyone who works with pixels for a living - this is a place to share your common interest and passion with other like-minded individuals in our neck of the woods.
